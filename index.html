Biography

I’m Ph.D student in Chair for Computer Aided Medical Procedures & Augmented Reality, Fakultät für Informatik in Technische Universität München for computer vision and machine learning.

Currently co-supervised by Dr. Federico Tombari and Prof. Dr. Nassir Navab. with a topic of Development of deep learning techniques for representation and processing of 3D data for complex visual tasks.

I get master degree in Pattern Recognition and Intelligent System Laboratory from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, supervised by Dr. Weihong Deng. Two years are spent on research for biomedical information analysis and 3D vision tasks in BUPT. Additionally, I’m also track and field athlete in rank B League of Beijing City.

Several academic projects were sponsored by Microsoft and Google open source funding, two Google Summer of Codes projects are supervised by mentors in OpenCV which is tiny-dnn and 3D object pose estimation.
